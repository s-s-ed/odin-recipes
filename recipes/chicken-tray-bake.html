Chicken and Rice Traybake

Home

The oven does all the work with this aromatic, spicy coconut rice tray bake. The chicken cooks to a wonderful tenderness and the rice is fluffy and totally delicious.

Ingredients

Spice Mix

  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp ground tumeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • sea salt

Tray Bake

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • 1 brown onion, diced
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp crushed ginger
  • 1 1/3 C basmati rice, rinsed
  • 1 tin coconut milk (400ml)
  • 400ml chicken stock

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180C fan bake.
  2. Heat butter and oil in large lidded oven proof frying pan and brown chicken all over. Transfer the chicken to a plate.
  3. Combine the spice mix ingredients in a small bowl.
  4. Add the onion, garlic, ginger and spice mix to the pan and cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Stir in the rice, coconut milk and stock.
  6. Add the chicken to the pot.
  7. Put the lid on and cook for 25 minutes, then remove lid and cook for a further 15 minutes until the rice and chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Serve with green beans or a salad.
